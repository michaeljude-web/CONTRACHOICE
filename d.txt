# ContraChoice: A Modern Birth Control Information System to Improve Contraceptive  
Knowledge and Decision-Making among Women at SEAIT


## 4.0 Features and Functionalities

### Contraceptive Comparison Guide
Allows users to compare different birth control methods side by side in one place.  
It shows details like effectiveness, side effects, cost, and how each method is used, making  
it easier to choose without getting overwhelmed.

### Personalized Questionnaire
Users answer a few simple questions about themselves. The systemn then shows only  
the information that is most relevant to their age, health, and lifestyle instead of showing  
everything at once.

### Anonymous Q&A Community Forum
This feature is like a private online discussion board where women can post  
questions about contraceptives without using their real name or identity. Other users or  
moderators can reply with answers and experiences. This feature is important because many Women feel shy or embarrassed to ask about reproductive health in person. The anonymity  
SIves them the freedom to ask anything without fear of being judged or identified.

This is an automated virtual assistant built into the system that is available anytime,

### Smart Contraceptive Recommendation Engine
This feature automatically suggests the most suitable birth control methods based
